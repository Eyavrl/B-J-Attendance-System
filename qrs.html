B&J Aircon Services — Employee QR Cards

Loads employees from your Google Sheet and creates scannable QR codes that encode only the Employee ID (e.g. 042023-14).

← Back to setup Open scanner
Paste your Web App URL and click Load employees.
How to use
  1. Paste your Web App /exec URL above and click Load employees.
  2. Click Print all QR cards or Download all PNGs and give each employee their card.
  3. Open the scanner, tap Activate scanner, and scan a card — attendance records to SHEET_LOG.
  4. Optional: In Google Apps Script, run syncEmployeeQrFormulas() once to auto-fill QR images in the EMPLOYEES tab column D.
